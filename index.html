What is the purpose of our club?

With the development of technology, students are likely to engage in the internet but ignore the significance of social humanity, which won't boost the progress of social development indeed. As a result, we set up the club to share half an hour every week, discussing a variety of social topics and debating with each other.

How to apply for the club?

Email is the best way to submit your application to us. Our official email: socialclub.westlake@gmail.com

Are there any recommended readings before joining the club?

Sure. We sincerely recommend reading the first 3 chapters of Capital: A Critique of Political Economy, written by Karl Marx.
